# MODULUS — "POWER UP"
### Suno-ready theme song · ~60 seconds · arcade epic

A 60-second arcade power-anthem. Insert-coin energy, neon synths, fist-pump chorus.
The story in one breath: **unlock your mind → master the four functions → stay true to
what's inside you → grow stronger → become the best.** Triumphant, fast, heroic — the song
that plays when the hero powers up and the high score finally falls.

---

## 1) STYLE PROMPT  (paste into Suno's "Style of Music")

```
epic arcade anthem, 80s synthwave power-anthem, bright driving synth arpeggios, punchy
electronic drums, chiptune accents, soaring heroic gang-vocal chorus, triumphant and fast,
retro-future, major key with a huge anthemic lift, fist-pump energy, ~140 BPM
```

**Title field:** `MODULUS — Power Up`
**Exclude Styles:** `slow, ballad, sad, lo-fi, mumble, downtempo`

> **Getting it to ~60s:** turn OFF extend / keep it a single take, and use the short structure
> below (one verse, one chorus, a short chant, a final chorus). Suno lands close to a minute on
> a compact lyric like this; trim the tail if it runs a few seconds over.

## 3) TIPS

- **Hook to protect:** "Unlock your mind — go higher, higher!" If Suno buries it, use **Replace Section** on the chorus.
- **The function chant** is the signature. If Suno blurs "PLUS / MINUS / MULTIPLY / DIVIDE," try the tag `[chant, staccato, gang vocals]` on that section.
- **Too soft?** add `aggressive, high-energy, distorted synth`. **Too noisy?** add `melodic, clean, heroic`.
- **For the trailer:** this 60s anthem can sit under a 60s cut directly, or trim to your trailer length — drop the "Power up!" chorus hit on the climax / K.O. and let the Outro stinger land on the logo.
