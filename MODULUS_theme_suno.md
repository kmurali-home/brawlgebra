# MODULUS — "More Than Zero"
### Suno-ready theme song

An anthem for a 15-year-old: **your mind can unlock more than you ever dreamed possible.**
Math isn't the homework — it's the key. Every problem you solve opens a door you didn't know
was there and levels you up. The villain, **Solomon ("you'll end at zero")**, is really just
*doubt* — the voice that says you're nothing, you'll stay small. The whole song is the kid
proving it wrong. Still MODULUS-true (solve, aura, zero), but personal and hype, not mythic.
**Modulus = |x| = how far you can go from zero** — that's the hook.

## 5) TRAILER CUT (~30s) — length & sync

**The full song above is ~2:30–3:00. The trailer is ~30s. They are NOT the same length — and they shouldn't be.**
You don't length-match a song to a trailer; you take the best ~30s window (a build → one drop → a tag) and land the drop on the K.O. frame. Two ways to get there:

- **Easiest (recommended):** generate the full song, then trim a 30s window in editing and sync the bass-drop to the K.O. I have `ffmpeg` here — hand me the exported audio + I'll cut it to length and align the hit.
- **Purpose-built short:** paste the trimmed structure below so Suno makes ~45–60s you can tighten. (Suno won't hit exact 30s, so still trim the final.)

**Beat map — music ↔ trailer (~30s):**

| Trailer moment | ~Time | Music |
|---|---|---|
| Cold open / hook (the math→punch) | 0–6s | ominous tick + whispered "Reduce. Resolve.", low choir, sparse |
| Logo + fighter reveals | 6–13s | drums enter, the build, tension climbing |
| Mechanic / modes + Solomon menace | 13–19s | riser, choir swell, near-peak |
| **Beat of silence** before the finish | ~19–20s | drop everything to a single held note |
| **K.O. — the impact** | ~20–21s | **THE DROP**: bass hit + choir stab lands on the flash |
| Title + "PLAY FREE" CTA | 21–29s | anthem hook holds under the logo |
| Final frame | 29–30s | one voice — "more than I know" → breath/silence |

**Rule of thumb:** whatever the final trailer length, there should be exactly **one** drop, and it goes on the K.O. Everything before is build; everything after is the tag.
