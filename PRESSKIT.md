## ABOUT THIS GAME (long description body)

**Your answers are ammo.**

BRAWLGEBRA is a fighting game with one twist that changes everything: every punch, kick, and special costs AURA — and the only way to refill AURA is to **solve math, mid-fight, under pressure.** Run your bank dry and you physically cannot attack. Think fast and the whole arena is yours.

This isn't a quiz with a health bar taped on. The math *is* the economy. A cornered opponent with an empty bank is helpless — so you can play to starve them out. A telegraphed heavy strike opens a split-second **NOW window**: answer the flashing problem in time and you don't just block it, you **slip clean through it** and stagger them. Every mechanic a real fighter has — combos, cancels, launchers, throws that beat block, wake-up reversals — is here, and every one of them runs on your ability to do arithmetic faster than the other guy.

**Four sworn Orders. One war over every number.**

Fight through a fourteen-chapter story campaign against the Orders of the Plus, Minus, Times, and Divide — each a fighter with a signature style and a special move built from their operator — up to SOLOMON, the Equals, who intends to reduce every answer, and every fighter, to nothing.

**MODES**

- **STORY** — a fourteen-chapter campaign; win chapters to unseal each Order as a playable fighter.
- **THE TOWER** — climb all five rivals, then the final boss. Lose and you retry that rung.
- **GAUNTLET** — an endless streak; every win comes back harder.
- **DAILY FIGHT** — one seeded challenge a day, same for everyone, with a shareable result.
- **ONLINE** — 1v1 duels against a friend by room code, or a 5-player rumble; AI fills empty seats.
- **LOCAL** — same-screen 2P and 5-fighter rumble.
- **SPARRING** — a training dummy to drill on.

**FEATURES**

- Answers are ammo: a math economy that drives real fighting-game decisions, not a bolted-on quiz.
- Six fighters, each with a distinct signature special and play-style.
- Answer-to-slip: turn a perfect read into a hit that passes right through you.
- Pick your level — quick sums to algebra — so the fight fits the fighter.
- Free in your browser. Nothing to install to try it.

**Do math. It's the most powerful thing you own.**
